INTERNATIONAL JAVASCRIPT CONFERENCE 2022

The IJS Conference brings together a vibrant community of experts,academics and activist who comprise the Creative Commons affialiate network in a different country every two years.
This year,we hope to expand our invitation list including organization and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons,free culture and open knowledge.
So if you're active and engaged in the world of open content konwledge - free software advocates ,Wikipedian , Open Knownledge, galleries,libraries,museums,archives,goverments and foundations, lawyers , and avtivist - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about IJS Conference 2022 !
